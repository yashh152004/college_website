COMPUTER ENGINEERING

A NEW DIMENSION IN THE FIELD OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

THE GOAL OF COMPUTER ENGINEERING IS TO DEVELOP THE EFFICIENT AND RELIABLE USE OF COMPUTERS AND COMPLEX HARDWARE AND SOFTWARE SYSTEMS THAT PROVIDE SOLUTIONS FOR REAL-WORLD PROBLEMS THIS INVOLVES EVERYTHING, FROM THE DESIGN TO THE DEVELOPMENT PROCESS AND FROM HARDWARE AND SOFTWARE INTEGRATION TO TESTING AND EVALUATION. THE FIELD OF COMPUTER ENGINEERING REQUIRES A FOUNDATION OF COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING BASICS. COMPUTER ENGINEERING IS OFTEN DEFINED AS THE DISCIPLINE OF EMBEDDED SYSTEMS.

COMPUTER SCIENCE IS THE STUDY OF COMPUTERS AND COMPUTATIONAL SYSTEMS AND THE USE OF SOFTWARE ALGORITHMS TO HELP SOLVE COMPLEX COMPUTING PROBLEMS.

  • HARDWARE COMPUTER ENGINEERS DESIGN AND DEVELOP COMPUTER SYSTEMS, CIRCUIT BOARDS, COMPUTER CHIPS, AND RELATED EQUIPMENT.
  • SOFTWARE COMPUTER ENGINEERS CREATE THESE TOOLS THAT ARE ABLE TO MANAGE THE OPERATIONS AND DESIGN OF COMPUTERS BY MANAGING SOFTWARE, SYSTEMS, AND NETWORKS AS WELL AS BY WRITING CODE.
